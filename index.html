Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the surge predictions?
Our AI achieves 78% accuracy for surge predictions within a 30-minute window and 85% accuracy for 2-hour predictions. We continuously improve our models with real-world data from thousands of drivers.
Is my data safe and private?
Absolutely. We only access publicly available APIs and never store your personal information, trip details, or passenger data. All data is encrypted and processed locally on your device where possible.
Which platforms does TaxiFlow AI work with?
Currently, we support Uber, Lyft, and Ola across Auckland, Wellington, Sydney, Melbourne, and Brisbane. We're adding more platforms and cities based on driver demand.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties. Your account will remain active until the end of your current billing period, and you can reactivate whenever you want.
How much can I realistically expect to earn extra?
Most full-time drivers see 15-25% increase in earnings, which translates to $200-400 extra per week. Part-time drivers typically see $50-120 extra weekly. Results vary based on market conditions and how closely you follow recommendations.
